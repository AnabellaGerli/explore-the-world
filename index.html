Wanderlust: The wish to travel far away and to many different places

    How to start traveling
  • Find the reason you want to travel
  • Imagine yourself travelling
  • Make a list of the places you want to go
  • Decide what kind of trip you want to do, whether is low budget or first class, conecting with people or conecting with the story and architecture of the place
  • Look for information online about the countries you want to visit, their visas and requirements

Let's explore!

Let's go to the mountains

Spending actively a few days in the mountains will get your body in perfect shape and your mood will naturally improve – there’s scientifically based fact that outdoors physical exercise produces loads of endorphins (“happy hormones”).

Let's go to the beach

The expansive view of the ocean water, often referred to as “blue space” by psychologists, actually calms our mind. If you've ever sat staring at an ocean and found that your problems seem to melt away as a sense of peace overcomes you, you're not alone. We are biologically hardwired to experience the beach

Let's go to the city

We love the city. We love watching people, we love the buzz that moves through buildings, parks and squares like an airflow. The city is home to multiple conformities. In a city it all happens; a microcosmos of opportunities and historic failures.
